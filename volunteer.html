WHAT VOLUNTEERS DO

On a typical regatta day, there are over 50 volunteer shifts to be filled by parents, and our Crew families are famous for the way they pitch in at regattas. Whether you opt to shop for fixins, pitch the tent at sunrise, flip pancakes, scramble eggs, tote supplies, sling chili, set out salads, or videotape the races, you'll find that volunteering is a great way to meet other families and learn about crew. C'mon, sign up. You'll have fun and feel great about supporting the team!


NEW THIS YEAR: SIGN-UP THROUGH OUR VOLUNTEER FORM!

To make volunteer sign-up easier, we have a new method: Volunteer Sign-Up Form. All it requires is your name and email address, then you're ready to go!

What are the advantages?

  • Works great on your cellphone.
  • Lets you sign up directly for the spot you want.
  • Lets you see who else has signed up.
  • Keeps track of your slots so you can check easily, any time.
